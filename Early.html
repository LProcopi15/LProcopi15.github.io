The Early Years

I was born in Gloucester, Virginia which is a small town near Williamsburg. I had the typical American life with two sisters, three dogs and a cat.
When I was two years old my family moved to Germany for my dad's work. Living in Germany was a life changing experiece. I went to a British preschool and traveled all over Europe during the summer.
This amazing experience gave me the desire to travel the world and experience other cultures. Since then I have gone back to Europe twice and loved every minute of it.
In middle school I traveled back to Germany with my soccer team. We traveled around Germany playing against the local teams and enjoying the tourist attractions. Then, in high school I traveled through Sweden, Denmark, Germany and France for the summer.
